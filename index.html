MOUNTAINS

From hills to the heavens

In the caves and cliffs update, one of the major features is the new mountains. Having increased the world height from 256 blocks to 320 blocks, there is a lot more space for the 6 new mountain biomes. These range from the , , , , and (see to the right for more information on each biome).

MEADOW

The meadow is a warmer biome of the mountains. It features a turquoise-green hue of grass with patches of flowers and grass. Here sheep, donkey, and rabbits can all spawn. Flowers, other than the biome specific or special flowers, can all generate here as well. Occasionally a lone tree will generate with a bee nest, a good place to find your first bee nest in a new world. This is also the only mountain biome where villages (plains) can generate, in addition to pillager towers. The meadow can be found either at the lower slopes of mountains or as a stand alone plateau.

GROVE

Mountain tops many times do not have trees on top, however with the grove biome, a forest of spruce trees generate on the slopes of a mountain. Here in this cold biome, water freezes and the surface is covered with snow and also the new powder snow. The grove is the home of Rabbits, wolves and also foxes. It will also generate next to forests and taigas.

SNOWY SLOPES

The snowy slopes biome is a transitional biome between the lower levels of the mountains and the peaks. It is covered in snow and is the home to goats and rabbits. A special structure, igloos, also have a chance to spawn in this biome. The biome generates next to plains biomes, however can also generate next to a snowy tundra.

JAGGED PEAKS

One of the three peak biomes is the jagged peaks. It will only generate on the top of mountains if they are tall enough for it to generate. The biome generates in snowy, cold biomes and will often generate jagged peaks, hence the name, which can surpass the clouds at y=256. Only the adventurous goat lives in these biomes, however ores such as iron, coal and emerald can be found while climbing on the sides of the jagged peaks.

FROZEN PEAKS

One of the three mountain peak biomes is the frozen peaks. They are covered in snow and with glaciers of packed ice. The thick furred goats are the only mob that spawn in this biome. This biome will usually generate next to smooth cold hill biomes however occasionally will generate in more jagged terrains.

STONY PEAKS

The warmer mountain peak biome is the stony peaks. It mainly generates by warm biomes such as savannas and jungles and it will no generate by snowy slopes or groves. The stony peaks, as the name suggests, are covered in stone, with veins of calcite and exposed ores. The grass in the stony peaks have a unique vibrant light green tint. Sadly, no passive mob has the stony peaks as a home.

Miscellaneous

Anything and Everything Else

Other major changes that will be incorporated into the game including:
• Huge changes to the terrain limits, changing the lowest Y-axis block to -64 and lowering the highest block to 320!
• Adjusted and wider rivers, as well as more fjords cutting through mountains.
• Bundles will be introduced which allows for the creation of bags to help inventory management.
• Ore veins making longer and rarer ore formations.
• The introduction of the blind mob that detects players through footsteps, called the Warden that is sure to give you a shock!
